Genshin Impact: How to unlock Pyro Traveler

This guide contains spoilers for the Natlan Archon Quest, so readers should proceed with caution.
By Syakil
21 November 2024

As Genshin Impact’s Natlan hit servers, many players expected to receive the Pyro Traveler along with the release. In previous regional releases, players could unlock the respective elemental Traveler by resonating with the Statue of Seven upon arrival. However, it’s not as simple as it seems in Genshin Impact’s Natlan.


Unfortunately, players have to wait until the end of Act 5 of the Archon Quest to receive the Pyro Traveler and their constellations. The ability is heavily story locked, and this guide will cover how players can finally obtain it and get their true constellations.

Traveler constellations

Once players reach Natlan, they will need to complete the entirety of the Archon Quest to unlock the Pyro Traveler. Incandescent Ode of Resurrection is the fifth and final Act of the chapter, and once players reach the point of the story titled “Where All Hopes Lie,” only then will they be one step closer to unlocking the Pyro Traveler. The ability will automatically unlock during the Gosoythoth boss fight, and players can use that chance to try it out.


How to Unlock Pyro Traveler's True Constellations In Genshin Impact

While the Traveler does receive all six constellations during the Gosoythoth boss fight, unfortunately, these are not their true constellations. You need to use your Blazing Flint Ore at the Throne of Primal Fire to unlock the Pyro Travelers' true constellations.


The Throne of Primal Fire is located in the middle of Ochkanatlan, and you can reach the area by teleporting to the waypoint near it and gliding down below to find it. Furthermore, you need to be the Pyro Traveler in order to offer the Blazing Flint Ore to the throne and obtain each constellation.


The only way Genshin Impact players can obtain Blazing Flint Ore is by maxing all the Reputation levels of each Natlan tribe. They can teleport to the Reputation waypoint and check all available things to do for this. These range from bounties, quests, exploration, and more. Once players reach Reputation Level 4, that’s only when they’ll be able to claim the Blazing Flint Ore.
